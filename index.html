Molly's Irish Pub

You can find our little pub on the corner of East Chestnut and North Shippen street, just accross from the beautiful Musser Park in downtown Lancaster, Pa.
Molly's Pub has all the comfort and charm of a traditional Irish pub. The beautiful hardwood bar and cozy dining room create an atmospere perfect for any reason to gather. Weather its a lunch with coworkers, drinks with friends, or dinner with family, our welcoming atmosphere and accomadating staff makes Molly's feel like a second home.
Our menu offers a varied selection of all your favorites at value minded prices.
